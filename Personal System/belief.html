How will I bring value to the world?

As the elderly always has difficulties or mental barriers in using technology, so I want to create experience, which interactivity is humanized, to allow people who seldom use techonology or reject using technology before to enjoy the advantages of technology. Also, people with disability also have their own problems in using technological interfaces, so I want to create designs that are responsive to the needs of a wide range of people.

How will I impact others in a positive way?

I want to create user-interfaces that are related to the life of elderly and disability. Through this method, I hope to increase their interest on the using technology, which can improve their life quality.

What are you passionate about?

I am passionate about designing layouts and experiences connecting between human and non-human things, such as paper and computer. I like to see people feeling pleasant and satisfied on the improvement of life through good design.

What are you skilled at doing?

I do not think I have any well-developed skill at this point but I want to develop coding skills so I can implement more design ideas which can achieve my goals.

How can you make money?

I think I can work for design firm after graduate.

What companies would you love to work for?

I would like to work for companies which value social equality and humanity, rather than gaining revenue. If there is an user experience agency that mainly work for NGOs, I would like to work in it.
