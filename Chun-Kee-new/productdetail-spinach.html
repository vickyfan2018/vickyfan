烹調方法

Cooking Method

湯麵

Noodle Soup

  • 1. 先將面用凍水浸約四至六分鐘

  • 2. 放入沸水中(一碗半)煮約2 分鐘, (時間長些,麵條較軟滑; 時間短些, 麵調較爽), 將麵條鬆開, 加入蔬菜、肉類、熟油及適量配味, 調成上湯, 即可食用。

  • 1. Soak the noodles with frozen water for about four to six minutes.

  • 2. Put in boiling water (one bowl and a half) and cook for about two minutes. (Longer time, the noodles are softer and smoother; The shorter the time, the cooler the noodles). Loose noodles, add vegetables, meat, cooked oil, and appropriate amount of taste, and turn them into soup.

撈麵

Lo mein

  • 如上述湯麵煮法, 將麵條煮開後, 撈起加入熟油及撈麵調味(蠔油或豉油等)即可食用

  • Using the method for cooking soup noodle, after the noodles are cooked, seasoned the noodles with cooked oil and noodle seasoning (eg, oyster sauce or oyster sauce).

炒麵

Fried noodle

  • 1.如上述湯麵煮法, 將已煮的麵條撈起,瀝乾水分。

  • 2. 將肉類蔬菜及配料等炒至快熟後, 加入麵條及適量調味炒勻即可食用。

  • 1. Using the soup noodle cooking method above, pick up the boiled noodles and drain the water.

  • 2. After frying the meat and vegetables and other ingredients until they are cooked, add noodles and seasoning and stir to mix.

微波爐煮法

Microwave cooking method

  • 1. 將麵餅放入大湯碗中, 先用凍水浸二至三分鐘, 然後將整碗麵連水放入爐中, 用高火煮兩分鐘。

  • 2. 待微波爐停止操作後, 取出湯碗用筷子將面鬆開, 再放入微波爐中,用高火煮一分鐘。

  • 3. 然後取出湯麵, 加入少許麻油 配料及適量調味料即可食用

  • 1. Place the noodle in a big soup bowl and soak in cold water for two to three minutes. Then put the whole bowl of noodles into the oven and cook it on high heat for two minutes.

  • 2. After the microwave oven stops operating, take out the soup bowl and release it with chopsticks, put it in the microwave, and cook for one minute with high heat.

  • 3. Then remove the noodles, add a little sesame oil, and the appropriate seasoning to eat
