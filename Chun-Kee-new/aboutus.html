About Us

春記由樊春先生1965年在西灣河136號地下創立。他使用廣州傳統的造麵技術製造麵條,春記很快便打出名堂。

Chun Kee was founded by Mr. Fan Chun in 1965 on the ground floor of No. 136 Sai Wan Ho. He used the traditional techniques of making pasta in Guangzhou to make noodles.

春記其後於1975年遷於西灣河興民街30號。以上相片便是當時遷鋪開幕的狀況。

Chun Kee was subsequently relocated to 30 Hing Man Street, Sai Wan Ho in 1975. The above photo is the opening of the shop at the time.

樊春離世後, 第五及第七兒子將麵廠的重擔接棒, 子承父業, 一直遵從父親的教誨,用傳統和嚴謹的製麵方式, 選用優質材料, 工序一絲不苟, 用心製作麵條。

After Fan Chun passed away, the fifth and seventh sons took over the burden of the noodle factory and inherited his father’s father’s teachings. They use traditional and rigorous methods of making, select high-quality ingredients, and meticulously craft their noodles.
